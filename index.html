A True Kannadiga hotel for culture lovers!

Benne dose

Benne dosa or benne dose is a type of dosa which traces its origin from the city of Davangere in Karnataka, India.

Mangluru Bajji

Mangalore Bonda, Mangalore Bajji, or Goli Baje, whatever you prefer to call them, are crispy, fried snacks that are firm on the outside and soft, melt-in-the-mouth on the inside.

Akki rotti

Akki rotti means "rice bread" in the native language, Kannada. It is made of rice flour which is mixed with salt and water and kneaded well till the dough gets soft.
